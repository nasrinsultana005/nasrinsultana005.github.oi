About Me

I am a researcher and engineer at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Industrial Systems. My academic journey spans two continents — beginning with a B.S. in Electrical & Electronics Engineering in Dhaka, Bangladesh, and continuing with an M.S. in IT Management in the United States (expected 2026).

My research is published across 11 peer-reviewed journals and IEEE conferences, accumulating over 150 citations. Topics include machine learning for intrusion detection, multimodal AI for misinformation detection, adaptive education systems, and AI-enhanced network security for 6G.

Beyond academia, I have practical field experience managing industrial electrical infrastructure, PLC/HMI/VFD systems, IT networks, and production engineering — giving me a rare ability to bridge hardware-level engineering with modern AI-driven software solutions.

I am also a Peer Reviewer for the International Journal of Applied Mathematics (IJAM) and a former national-level competitive footballer, bringing discipline and team-leadership to everything I do.
